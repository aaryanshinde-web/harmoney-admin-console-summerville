# Manage Business: Search & Business Profile

> Find a business, open its profile, confirm the basics.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Search for a Business

Accepts exact Business ID or partial Business Name.

![Step 1: Search for a Business](img/mb-01-search-default.png)

### Step 2: Search results

Every matching business. Labels (ACH Origination, Privileged, demo) show on each result card.

![Step 2: Search results](img/mb-02-search-results-llc.png)

### Step 3: Business Profile

Single pane of glass. Left navigator: General Information, Business Permissions, Business Limits, User Roles, Recipients, Approval Settings, Business Users.

![Step 3: Business Profile](img/mb-03-business-profile-landing.png)

### Step 4: Overview and Support

Dropdown. Overview = read-only facts. Support = servicing actions staff can run.

![Step 4: Overview and Support](img/mb-04-overview-support-dropdown.png)

### Step 5: General Information

Legal name, mailing address, core-system IDs. Confirm it matches the tax forms and banking agreement before changing anything.

![Step 5: General Information](img/mb-05-general-information.png)

## Summary

Entry point to Manage Business. Search > Profile > General Information. Always confirm the legal-name / core-ID match before any entitlement change.

## Key Use Cases

- Loan-closing ticket has the Business ID: ID search, straight to profile.
- Walk-in, partial name only: Name search, confirm via labels and General Information.
