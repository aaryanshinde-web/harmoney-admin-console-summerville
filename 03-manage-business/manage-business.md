# Manage Business

_Manage Businesses_

> The full set of staff actions on a business profile — find a business, then configure its permissions, limits, roles, recipients, approvals, and users from one screen.

The pages in this module cover the search flow at the top, then everything reached by drilling into a Business Profile, plus the credit-union-wide catalogue pages (Labels, Permission Templates, Role Templates).

## In this module

**Business Profile drill-down**

* **Business Search & Profile** — find a business and open its profile, including General Information.
* **Business Permissions & Limits** — feature catalogue and dollar ceilings.
* **Business User Roles** — per-business roles with permissions and limits.
* **Business Recipients & Approval Settings** — saved external payees and dual-control approver counts.
* **Business Users** — every employee on the business with their applied role, permissions, and limits.

**Credit-union-wide catalogue**

* **Business Labels** — segmentation tags for campaigns, risk sweeps, and pricing.
* **Onboarding & Permission Templates** — onboard a new business and maintain entitlement bundles.
* **Role Templates** — pre-built role definitions every business seeds from.
