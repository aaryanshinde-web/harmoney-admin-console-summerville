# Manage Business — Labels

> The segmentation vocabulary behind Treasury campaigns, risk sweeps, and pricing reviews.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1 — Manage Labels

The catalogue — ACH Origination, LLC business, Platinum, Privileged, demo. Treat it like CRM tags: owned, documented, pruned.

![Step 1 — Manage Labels](img/mb-23-manage-labels-list.png)

### Step 2 — Label Details

Open a label for every business tagged with it, each link-clickable into its profile. Fan out from tag to business, not the other way.

![Step 2 — Label Details](img/mb-24-label-details.png)

### Step 3 — Remove Label

Confirmation modal. Removing a label affects every business and every downstream campaign — pause journeys first.

![Step 3 — Remove Label](img/mb-25-remove-label-confirm.png)

### Step 4 — Add Label

From a Business Profile, attach one or more catalogue labels to this entity. Picked up immediately by reports and journeys.

![Step 4 — Add Label](img/mb-26-add-label-modal.png)

## Summary

Labels are the segmentation backbone. Campaigns, reports, and journeys pivot on them. Catalogue lives in Manage Labels; per-business tagging lives on the profile.

## Key Use Cases

- Positive-pay campaign for LLC ACH originators → open ACH Origination label, hand the list to Treasury.
- Client promoted to Privileged tier → Business Profile → Add Label → Privileged.
