# Manage Members — Overview & Support

> Member Information + a full audit trail of every admin action.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### Step 1 — Overview and Support

The first blue pill under Profile Actions. Holds Member Information and Support History.

![Step 1 — Overview and Support](img/mm-11-overview-support-dropdown.png)

### Step 2 — Support History

Every admin action on this member, in order, with Admin Comment and who did it. Check this first on any second-touch call.

![Step 2 — Support History](img/mm-12-support-history.png)

## Summary

The default section when you open a member profile. Two panels: Member Information (identity) and Support History (chronological audit of every reset, lock, block, unenroll).

## Key Use Cases

- Second CSR on a callback → Support History shows what the first CSR did.
- Control review → Support History entries reconcile against change tickets.
