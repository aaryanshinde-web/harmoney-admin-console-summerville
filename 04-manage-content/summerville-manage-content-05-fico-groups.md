# Manage Content — FICO & Audience Groups

> Stage the FICO credit-score disclosures and enrolment copy, and maintain the content-segmentation groups that drive audience targeting for banners, notifications, and terms.

## Summary

FICO & Audience Groups are the two Manage Content surfaces that govern how Summerville reaches a specific slice of its member base. FICO is the content block for the credit-score module — the vendor-mandated disclosure text and the enrolment copy that governs how members opt in — a compliance-sensitive block that Marketing and Compliance jointly own.

Add Group maintains the content-segmentation groups that drive audience targeting for banners, notifications, and terms. Groups are how Summerville cuts its member base into Treasury-only, commercial-only, consumer-only, or state-specific audiences — and they are what lets a single operator run a California-only notification or a Treasury-only banner without building a new channel for every campaign.

## Key Use Cases

Summerville adds FICO score enrolment to the commercial dashboard. The operator opens the FICO surface, stages the vendor-mandated disclosure text and the enrolment copy Compliance has signed off, and the opt-in flow is live for commercial members on the next session.

Treasury wants to run a targeted advisory only for businesses in California subject to a state-specific regulation change. The operator opens Add Group, provisions or picks the California segment, and the Notifications and Terms & Conditions surfaces can now target that segment directly rather than broadcasting to the whole book.

## End-to-End Workflow

### Prerequisites

- Manage Content role assigned to the operator via Manage Administrators (separate roles for Marketing, Compliance, and Content Operations where the bank has chosen to split duties).
- Approved marketing creative (1000x1296 banner image), approved legal document (PDF for Terms and Conditions), or approved broadcast copy for Notifications, reviewed and signed off outside the console.
- For scheduled content: a confirmed effective date or send timestamp in Pacific Standard Time, aligned with the regulatory, campaign, or operations calendar.
- For audience targeting: content-segmentation Groups already provisioned via Add Group so the operator can scope a banner or notification to the intended audience.
